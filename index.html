Transparency First

How We Separate Real Sustainability From Greenwashing

Every listing on EcoRenter goes through our evidence-based verification system. No resort pays for placement. No certification gets a free pass.

Tier A: Certified & Audited

Third-party certifications from recognized bodies like GSTC, Rainforest Alliance, or Green Globe. Independent audits verify claims. This is the gold standard.

Tier B: Documented Operations

Resorts that publish transparent operational data: energy consumption, water usage, waste metrics, community payments. Open books, verifiable numbers.

Tier C: Community Verified

Verified reports from travelers, local communities, and NGOs. On-the-ground evidence from people who have experienced the resort's actual practices firsthand.

Impact Scoring

Every listed resort receives a transparent impact score across five dimensions: energy, water, waste, community, and biodiversity. High scores require evidence, not promises.

Trade-Off Transparency

No resort is perfect. We document the trade-offs: the eco-lodge with great solar power but poor waste management. The truth, always, complete.

Never Published (Tier D)

Resorts that fail verification or refuse transparency are never listed. No amount of marketing budget can buy a listing on EcoRenter. Period.
