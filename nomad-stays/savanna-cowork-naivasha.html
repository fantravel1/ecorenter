Savanna Cowork Lake Naivasha: Your Lakeside Office in Kenya's Rift Valley

Work from a lakeside desk where hippos surface 30 meters away, fish eagles cry overhead, and 450 bird species turn every coffee break into a wildlife encounter. 60Mbps fiber, private cottages, and conservation funding built into every stay. Monthly from $900 in the Great Rift Valley.

Photo by Unsplash photographer. Lake Naivasha's wildlife-rich shores in Kenya's Great Rift Valley.

The Space

Your office soundtrack is fish eagles and hippos.

"I was writing a proposal when a hippo surfaced in the lake directly in front of my desk. Then an African fish eagle caught a tilapia not 50 meters away. My proposal was late, but my client understood when I sent the video. This is not a gimmick co-working space. It is a serious workspace that happens to be in one of Africa's most extraordinary ecosystems."

— Amina, Strategy Consultant, 3-month resident

Lake Naivasha is a freshwater jewel in Kenya's Great Rift Valley, supporting one of Africa's richest bird populations and a famous hippo community. The Savanna Cowork sits on the lakeshore within a community conservancy, combining professional-grade workspace with daily wildlife encounters. At 1,884 meters elevation, the climate is comfortable year-round (15-28C), avoiding the heat of lowland Kenya. Nairobi is 90 minutes away for city access when needed. The cowork channels 25% of revenue to lake conservation, making every productive workday also a contribution to environmental protection.

Lakeside Workspace

A stone and timber building with floor-to-ceiling windows facing the lake. 10 dedicated desks with ergonomic chairs and monitor stands. Hippos visible from every desk. A covered terrace extends the workspace outdoors with 4 additional stations. Solar-powered with battery backup. The building is elevated on a raised platform for flood protection and panoramic views.

Bird Photography Hides

Two professional bird photography hides at the water's edge, equipped with power outlets and WiFi, double as unique individual workstations. Ground-level observation of kingfishers, herons, and plovers. Camera rental available. These are the workspace's best-kept secret: incredibly productive due to enforced quiet and zero distractions (except occasional wildlife).

Private Cottages

Six lakeside cottages with private verandas overlooking the water. Ensuite bathrooms, comfortable beds, desk space, and personal WiFi coverage. Night sounds include hippos grazing on the lawn (they exit the lake after dark). Solar water heating. Mosquito nets. Each cottage has a unique view angle of the lake and surrounding acacia woodland.

Wildlife Experiences

Weekly boat safari on the lake among hippos, fish eagles, and papyrus islands. Crescent Island walking safari among giraffe and zebra. Hell's Gate National Park cycling trips through volcanic gorges. Lake Nakuru flamingo excursions. Night walks with spotlights for nocturnal wildlife. Bird checklists and reference guides provided for aspiring birders.
