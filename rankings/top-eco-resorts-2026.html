Top 50 Eco-Resorts 2026

The 50 highest-scoring eco-resorts worldwide, ranked by verified impact across energy, water, waste, community, and biodiversity. No resort pays for placement. Every score is evidence-backed.

2026 Edition

This year's rankings reflect a maturing industry, and rising standards.

The 2026 rankings evaluated over 2,000 eco-resorts across six countries. Average impact scores improved 4.7% year-over-year, driven largely by solar adoption and community benefit programs. Five new entrants join the Top 50, three resorts improved their evidence tier from B to A, and two former Top 10 resorts dropped due to expired certifications. The message is clear: sustainability is not a one-time achievement but an ongoing commitment.

2026 Insights

Key Trends This Year

Solar Adoption Surge

Average energy scores improved 6.2% across the Top 50, driven primarily by solar panel installations. 38 of 50 resorts now generate over 80% of their energy from renewable sources, up from 29 last year.

Community Scores Rising

Community impact scores improved 5.1% year-over-year as resorts formalize local employment programs and community benefit agreements. Kenya leads with an average community score of 91.3 across its Top 50 entries.

Water Remains a Challenge

Water dimension scores showed the smallest improvement (1.8%). Many resorts in water-stressed regions struggle with conservation infrastructure. This remains the biggest opportunity for improvement across the industry.

Evidence Quality Improving

12 resorts upgraded their evidence tier this year (10 from B to A, 2 from C to B). This reflects growing industry awareness that verified data matters more than marketing claims for credibility.
