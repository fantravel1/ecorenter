Eco-Resort Rankings

Transparent, evidence-based rankings of eco-resorts and sustainable destinations. No resort pays for placement. No certification gets a free pass. Just verified data, scored honestly.

Our Approach

Rankings should reflect reality, not marketing budgets.

"Most eco-resort rankings are paid placements or certification lists. Ours is neither. We score every resort across five sustainability dimensions using verified evidence, then rank them by their actual impact, not their advertising spend."

The travel industry's ranking systems are broken. Magazines rank resorts based on advertising relationships. Booking platforms rank based on commission rates. Certification bodies rank based on membership fees. EcoRenter ranks based on one thing only: verified sustainability impact. Our methodology is published, our data sources are documented, and our editorial team cannot be influenced by resort operators.

Full Transparency

Our Ranking Methodology

Every score in our rankings is derived from this transparent, reproducible methodology.

Dimension 1: Energy (20%)

Percentage of renewable energy use, energy efficiency measures, carbon intensity per guest night, and grid independence. Scored 0-100 based on documented energy data. Solar, wind, micro-hydro, and biogas systems receive higher scores than grid-dependent operations.

Dimension 2: Water (20%)

Water consumption per guest night, conservation measures, greywater recycling, rainwater harvesting, and wastewater treatment quality. Resorts in water-stressed regions receive adjusted baselines to account for regional water availability.

Dimension 3: Waste (20%)

Waste diversion rate, composting programs, single-use plastic elimination, zero-waste progress, and circular economy practices. Measured in kilograms of waste per guest night with higher scores for lower waste generation and higher diversion rates.

Dimension 4: Community (20%)

Local employment percentage, wage fairness (compared to regional averages), community benefit programs, cultural respect practices, and supply chain localization. This dimension ensures that environmental sustainability does not come at the expense of social justice.

Dimension 5: Biodiversity (20%)

Habitat protection, wildlife corridor maintenance, species monitoring programs, reforestation or restoration projects, and invasive species management. Scored based on documented conservation outcomes, not just policies.

Evidence Tier Multiplier

The evidence tier acts as a confidence multiplier: Tier A (1.0x) for independently verified data, Tier B (0.85x) for self-reported but documented data, Tier C (0.7x) for community-verified data. This ensures that resorts with stronger evidence backing rank appropriately higher.

Our Commitment

Ranking Transparency Principles

No Pay-for-Placement

No resort, destination, or certification body can pay for improved rankings, featured placement, or favorable scoring. Our revenue model is completely independent from our ranking system.

Published Methodology

Our complete scoring methodology is published on this page. Any changes to methodology are announced in advance and applied retroactively to ensure fair comparison across time periods.

Documented Trade-Offs

Every ranked resort includes documented trade-offs: areas where they excel and areas where they fall short. No resort is perfect, and our rankings reflect this nuance rather than hiding it.

Appeal Process

Resorts can submit additional evidence to improve their score at any time. Appeals are reviewed by our editorial team within 30 days. Successful appeals are reflected in the next quarterly update with full transparency about what changed and why.

Ranking Methodology FAQ

EcoRenter ranks eco-resorts using a composite impact score across five dimensions: Energy, Water, Waste, Community, and Biodiversity. Each is scored 0-100 based on verified evidence, then weighted equally (20% each) and combined. The evidence tier (A, B, or C) acts as a confidence multiplier.
