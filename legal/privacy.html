Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026  |  Effective Date: February 3, 2026

Summary: EcoRenter collects minimal data, uses only necessary cookies, does not track you across the web, does not sell your data, and does not serve advertising. We respect your privacy as much as we respect the planet.

1. Introduction

EcoRenter ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website ecorenter.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Site. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open, transparent manner.

By using our Site, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the Site.

2. Data We Collect

EcoRenter follows a principle of minimal data collection. We only collect data that is necessary for the functionality of our Site and services.

2.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

  • Newsletter Subscription: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. This is the only personal data we actively request.
  • Contact Forms: If you contact us via email or through our contact channels, we collect the information you choose to provide (name, email address, message content).
  • Community Contributions: If you submit eco-resort data, corrections, or other contributions, we collect the information included in your submission.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Server Logs: Our hosting provider may collect standard server log data including IP addresses, browser type, referring pages, and timestamps. This data is used solely for security and performance monitoring and is automatically deleted after 30 days.
  • Necessary Cookies: We use only cookies that are strictly necessary for the functioning of our Site (see Section 4 below).

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT use analytics tracking services (no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no third-party trackers)
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT create user profiles or behavioral profiles
  • We do NOT use advertising or remarketing technologies
  • We do NOT collect financial or payment information
  • We do NOT use fingerprinting or cross-site tracking

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Newsletter Delivery: To send our weekly sustainability and eco-resort updates to subscribers who have opted in
  • Responding to Inquiries: To reply to your questions, submissions, or feedback
  • Site Security: To protect our Site against malicious activity, unauthorized access, and security threats
  • Site Performance: To monitor and maintain the technical performance of our Site
  • Content Improvement: To understand aggregate usage patterns (not individual behavior) and improve our content

4. Cookies

EcoRenter uses only strictly necessary cookies. We do NOT use any tracking, analytics, advertising, or preference cookies.

4.1 Necessary Cookies We Use

  • Session cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. These are essential for basic site functionality.
  • PWA Service Worker: If you install EcoRenter as a progressive web app, service worker caching enables offline access to previously viewed content.

4.2 Cookies We Do NOT Use

  • No analytics cookies (Google Analytics, Matomo, etc.)
  • No advertising cookies (Google Ads, Facebook, etc.)
  • No social media tracking cookies
  • No cross-site tracking cookies
  • No preference or personalization cookies

5. No Tracking or Advertising

EcoRenter does not engage in any form of user tracking or advertising. Specifically:

  • We do not serve advertisements of any kind
  • We do not participate in advertising networks
  • We do not track your behavior across websites
  • We do not build behavioral profiles
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers
  • We do not use retargeting or remarketing technologies

Our revenue model is independent from advertising, which ensures our editorial independence and your privacy.

6. Email Newsletter

If you subscribe to our newsletter:

  • We store your email address securely for the sole purpose of sending our newsletter
  • We send emails no more than once per week
  • Every email includes a clear, one-click unsubscribe link
  • When you unsubscribe, your email address is permanently deleted within 7 days
  • We do not share your email address with any third parties
  • We do not purchase or use third-party email lists
  • We use a double opt-in process to confirm your subscription

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

EcoRenter does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. The limited exceptions are:

  • Hosting Provider: Our web hosting provider processes server log data as part of providing hosting services. They are contractually obligated to protect this data and not use it for any other purpose.
  • Email Service Provider: If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address is stored with our email service provider, who is contractually obligated to protect your data and process it only for newsletter delivery.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal process.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure storage with access controls for any personal data
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure
  • Minimal data collection to reduce risk exposure

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. GDPR Compliance

EcoRenter is committed to compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Economic Area (EEA). Under the GDPR:

9.1 Legal Basis for Processing

  • Consent: Newsletter subscription is based on your explicit consent (double opt-in)
  • Legitimate Interest: Server log processing for security and performance is based on our legitimate interest in maintaining a secure and functional website
  • Contractual Necessity: Processing contact form data is necessary to respond to your inquiry

9.2 Data Protection Officer

For GDPR-related inquiries, contact our data protection point of contact at: privacy@ecorenter.com

9.3 International Data Transfers

If your data is transferred outside the EEA, we ensure adequate safeguards are in place, including standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission.

10. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: You have the right to request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure: You have the right to request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You have the right to request restriction of processing of your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability: You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to processing of your personal data based on legitimate interest
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw consent at any time (e.g., unsubscribing from our newsletter)
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: privacy@ecorenter.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

11. Data Retention

  • Server Logs: Automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Newsletter Email Addresses: Retained until you unsubscribe, then permanently deleted within 7 days
  • Contact Form Data: Retained for up to 12 months to manage ongoing correspondence, then deleted
  • Community Contributions: Retained as long as the contributed content remains published, with personal identifiers removed upon request

12. Children's Privacy

EcoRenter does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 16. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete that information immediately. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 16, please contact us at privacy@ecorenter.com.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. For significant changes, we will provide notice through our newsletter (for subscribers) or a prominent notice on our Site.

14. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, your personal data, or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@ecorenter.com
  • General Inquiries: hello@ecorenter.com
  • Website: ecorenter.com
